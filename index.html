Privacy Policy

Roughin is a field tool for electricians built by LyfieldCreationsOS. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Short version: All job data, calculations, panel schedules, and inspection checklists are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. We do not collect, sell, or share your data.

Data Stored on Your Device

Roughin stores the following data locally on your device using on-device SQLite storage:

  • Job records (client name, address, job type, notes)
  • Electrical calculations and results
  • Panel schedules and circuit data
  • Inspection checklists and item statuses
  • Photos attached to checklist items
  • Material lists and price comparisons
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a PDF and share it using your device's share sheet. That sharing is handled entirely by your operating system — Roughin has no visibility into where you send it.

Camera and Photo Library

Roughin requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to attach photos to inspection checklist items. Photos are stored locally on your device in the app's document directory. They are not uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

Payments (Roughin Pro)

Roughin Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store in-app purchase system. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives your App Store purchase receipt and subscription status to verify your Pro entitlement. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, job data, or any other app content.

For details on how RevenueCat handles data, see their Privacy Policy. For Apple's data practices, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics and Tracking

Roughin does not use any analytics, tracking, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. We do not know how you use the app, how often you open it, or what features you use.

No Account Required

Roughin does not require an account. No email address, name, or personal information is collected at any point.

Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. To delete it, simply delete the Roughin app. All jobs, calculations, panels, checklists, and photos will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Roughin is intended for use by trade professionals. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
